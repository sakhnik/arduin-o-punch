# Bill of material

## Common parts

- RFID RC522
- Active buzzer
- LED
- Power Supply 18650 -> 3.3/5v
- Battery 18650
- Wires

## Arduino options

### Nano

- Arduino Nano 5v 16MHz
- Resistor 220R
- 4x Resistors 2k2
- 4x Resistors 1k

## RTC Clock options

### No RTC

Vulnerable to board reset.

### DS3132

Higher precision (TCOX), higher cost.

- RTC DS3132
- Battery 3.7v LIR2032
